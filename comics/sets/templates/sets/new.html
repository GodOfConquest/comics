Comic sets

A comics set is...

A comic set is a personal selection of comics. Anyone can create a new set, and anyone can modify any existing set, just like a wiki.

Getting started

To get started with your own personal comic set, enter a name for it, e.g. your username or nickname. To get to your comic set later, enter the name again, or go directly to the URL of your comic set.

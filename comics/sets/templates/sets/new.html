Comic sets

A comic set is a personal selection of comics. Anyone can create a new set, and anyone can modify any existing set, just like a wiki.

Getting started

To get started with your own personal comic set, enter a name for it below, e.g. your username or nickname. If the name is taken, choose another name. To get to your comic set later, enter the name again, or go directly to the URL of your comic set.
